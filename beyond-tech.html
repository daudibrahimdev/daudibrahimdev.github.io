Beyond Tech

FIRST HOBIES
First encounter with music

When I was five, my parents took me to a mall where I saw a boy playing the drums. I remember standing there, completely absorbed. Something about that moment stayed with me and quietly sparked the desire to try.

FIRST DRUM LESSON
Learning, briefly

I began taking drum lessons soon after. They did not last very long. The studio eventually closed, and my parents were unable to find another place at the time. Even so, the interest in music never really disappeared.

CHESS
Discovering chess

Around the same period, I spent a lot of time with the chess game on Windows 7. At first I did not understand the rules, so my father taught me how each piece moved and how a game unfolds. Years later, I finally won a match against him. It was a small moment, yet it pushed me to take the game more seriously and join several competitions.
